COVID-19 symptoms can vary in severity and onset time, typically appearing within 2-14 days after exposure to the virus. Common symptoms include fever, dry cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, muscle or body aches, headache, sore throat, loss of taste or smell, and occasionally, congestion or a runny nose. Gastrointestinal symptoms like nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea may also occur.
